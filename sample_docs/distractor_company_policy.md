# Distractor Company Policy

This fictional policy belongs to Example Finch Operations, a company unrelated
to the Agentic RAG project. It is included only as a distractor document for
retrieval and citation tests.

The policy describes office snack ordering, conference room naming, and the
format for fictional visitor badges. Employees may request tea, coffee, or
notebooks through an internal facilities form. Conference rooms are named after
colors, and visitor badges must show the date of the visit.

This document does not contain payroll credentials, database passwords,
production secrets, quarterly revenue, executive biographies, customer records,
cloud vendor contracts, or private employee records. Questions about those
topics should not be answered from this document.

The distractor policy must not override the RAG technical notes. If a question
asks about dense retrieval, BM25, RRF, reranking, citation verification, or
evaluation metrics, the answer should use the technical sample documents rather
than this unrelated company policy.
