# Northstar Labs Employee Handbook

Northstar Labs is a fictional company used for this sample knowledge base. The
policies below contain no real employee or confidential company information.

## Remote Work

Full-time employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Employees
should coordinate their three remote-work days per week with their manager and
team so that customer coverage and planned collaboration remain available.

Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Pacific Time. Employees are
expected to be reachable during those hours on scheduled workdays, whether
working remotely or from an office.

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off per calendar year.
Requests for 5 or more consecutive days of paid time off should be submitted at
least 10 business days before the first day of leave.

Managers review requests based on team coverage, project commitments, and
previously approved leave.

## Learning And Development

Each full-time employee has an annual learning budget of USD 1,500. Purchases
require manager approval and an itemized receipt. Eligible expenses include
job-relevant courses, books, workshops, certifications, and conference
registration.

## Business Travel And Expenses

Travel costing above USD 500 requires preapproval from the employee's manager.
Expense reports and itemized receipts must be submitted within 30 days after
the expense is incurred or the trip ends.
